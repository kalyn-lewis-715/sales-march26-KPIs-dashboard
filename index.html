📊 Inbound Lead Pipeline — CTA Analysis Note
Analysis of inbound lead flow from Aug 2025 through May 2026 reveals that site traffic degradation is the leading driver of pipeline decline — qualified lead volume has trended down from 42–46/month (Jun–Aug 2025) to 16–20/month (Mar–May 2026). The CTA button change has not materially altered conversion rates of leads through pipeline stages — the qualified-to-deal conversion rate has remained between 20–44% throughout. The pipeline weakness is a top-of-funnel volume problem, not a mid-funnel conversion problem.
